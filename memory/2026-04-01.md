## Automatic Seed Capture System
- **Real-time**: During conversations, write seeds to `memory/seeds-today.jsonl` (one JSON object per line: `{title, text, source, created}`)
- **Daily cron** (11 PM UTC): `daily_seed_extract.py` reads `seeds-today.jsonl` + extracts from `memory/YYYY-MM-DD.md`, deduplicates against Weaviate, creates via API or stages to `seeds-pending.jsonl`
- **Dedup**: Title similarity (>70% word overlap) against both Weaviate and staging files
- **What qualifies as a seed**: Strategic insights, product philosophy, career observations, surprising discoveries, reusable mental models
- **What's NOT a seed**: Tasks, questions, tactical execution, things already documented
- First 2 seeds captured today: "Product Thinking: Maximum Value, No Bloat" + "US Team Energy & FDE Bridge Opportunity"
